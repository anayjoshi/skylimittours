Royal Rajasthan with Taj and Goa

Destinations Covered in Tour

( 18 nights and 19 days)

Delhi: The Capital of India.(2 nights)

Agra: Famous for one of the seven wonders of world Taj Mahal.(1 night)

Jaipur: The Pink City & Capital of Rajasthan.(2 night)

Bikaner: Famous for Camels.(2 night) Jaisalmer The Golden Desert City.(3 night)

Jodhpur: The second Largest city of Rajasthan.(1 night)

Kumbhalgarh: Famous for Kumbhalgarh Fort.(1 night)

Udaipur: The City of Lakes.(2 night)

Goa: The Pearl of the East.(3 nights)

Mumbai: Business Capital of India.(1 night)

Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Arrive Delhi. Transfer to the hotel after late-evening arrival. Being the capital ofIndiathis state is said to be a part of modernIndiawhich is made up of the ancient cities. Overnight Stay will be at the hotel.

Day 02: Explore Delhi. After the breakfast in the morning you can visit the very prominent Red Fort, Qutub Minar and also the Humayun's Tomb. Overnight stay would be atDelhi.

Day 03: Delhi to Agra (210 Kms, 4-5 hrs drive) & Explore Agra. Drive will begin toAgrain the morning and after arrival atAgrayou will be given a transfer to the hotel. After rest you will be taken to the mesmerizing Taj Mahal and the Agra Fort. Overnight stay would be atAgra.

Day 04: Agra to Jaipur (240 Kms, 5-6 hrs drive). After breakfast drive to Jaipur, on the way you can take a glimpse of the Red Sandstone "Ghost Town" Fatehpur Sikri and also the very famous Ghana Bird Sanctuary of Bharatpur. Once you arrive to Jaipur then transfer to hotel and the overnight stay will be in Jaipur.

Day 05: Explore Jaipur. After your breakfast in the morning your journey for sight seeing commences and all different places like the Jaipur City Palace, Royal Observatory, Palace of Winds (Hawa Mahal), Amber Fort and Jaigarh Fort will be included here. Tour Activity You can enjoy the elephant ride at the Ambar fort. Shopping: Jaipur is known to be one of the best and also the most prominent places for the most brightly embroidered fabrics, blue pottery, silver jewelry, carpets and semi-precious stones. Evening Dinner: you can enjoy your evening dinner at Chokhi dani (an ethnic village resort) and here you will be able to see the village life of Rajasthan and also enjoy a few good traditional Rajasthani dances, puppet shows and other attention-grabbing activities.

Day 06: Jaipur to Bikaner (350 kms, 6-7 hrs drive). Drive to Bikaner commences in the morning, after reaching to t hat place your transfer will be done to the hotel and then stay overnight in Bikaner.

Day 07: Explore Bikaner. When you are done with your breakfast you can start with the visit to Junagarh,LalGarhPalace,GangaGoldenJubileeMuseum, Camel Research Farm and many more such places. Tour Activity You can enjoy the Camel Safari. Shopping: Mojari (Bikaneri style), rangi dupatta, Kundan work, wooden antiques, Lacquer Bangles, carpet making are the best materials which you will get here. The best work of art which is made out of camel hides is also said to be very prominent in Bikaner.

Day 08: Bikaner to Jaisalmer (325 kms, 6-7 hrs drive). Early morning drive to Jaisalmer will start, on the way you will be taken to see the very oldKarniMataTemple(Deshnok) and also the Gajner Wild Life Sanctuary. After reaching Jaisalmer in the evening you will get a transfer to the hotel. Over night stay would be at Jaisalmer.

Day 09: Explore Jaisalmer. When you are done with the breakfast, yoru tour to Jaisalmer will start and you will be able to take a look at the Jaisalmer Fort, Patwon ki Haveli,GadiSagarLakeand also the bestJainTemples. Stay over night in jaisalmer.

Day 10: Jaisalmer Tour. After your breakfast in the hotel, you can continue your tour of places in Jaisalmer, you can visit the Nathmalji Ki Haveli, Salin Singh Ki Haveli, Sam Sand Dunes & Khuri. Over night stay in jaisalmer. Travel Activity Camel Ride: this is one enjoyable Ride at the Sam sand dunes. Shopping: Jaisalmer is very prominent for the embroidery, Rajasthani mirror work, rugs, blankets, old stonework and antique. To the north of the city is the Kadi bhandar which gives you good dye and other fabrics.

Day 11: Jaisalmer to Jodhpur (270 Kms, 5-6 hrs drive). In the morning drive will commence towardsJodhpur, once you are there you will can visit the Mehrangarh Fort,JaswantThada & UmmaidBhawanPalace. Over night spent at Jodhpur.

Day 12: Jodhpur to Kumbhalgarh (270 kms, 5-6 hrs drive). After your breakfast in hotel drive commences to Kumbhalgarh. Here on the way you will be taken to visit famous good looking places like that of Ranakpur Jain Temples. Once you are done with this transfer to the hotel in Kumbhalgarh. Over night stay will be here.

Day 13: Travel Khumbhalgarh to Udaipur (90 kms, 2 hrs drive). You can take a look at the very magnificent Kumbhalgarh Fort andKumbhalgarhNational Parkafter your morning breakfast. As this completes your drive to Udaipur will start and you will get a transfer to the hotel there after you reachUdaipur. Your over night stay would be at this hotel.

Day 14: Explore Udaipur. After your breakfast you will be taken to see the beauty of theCityPalace, Saheliyon kiBari,JagdishTemple, Ahar, Sajjan Garh,PicholaLake,BhartiyaLokKalaMuseum, Shilpgram,EklingjiTemple. After the whole day journey rest at the hotel and then the over night stay will also be in Udaipur. Travel Activity Shopping Udaipurhas various different small shops and many interesting local crafts, particularly paintings of the Rajputs - Moghuls style.

Day 15: Travel to Goa. In the morning fly to Goa via Mumbai. After you reaching there you will get a transfer to the hotel and will then have a overnight stay at the hotel in Goa.

Day 16: Experience the Goa You will be taken to visit the various churches after you finish the morning breakfast and you will also be able to have a glimpse of the many historical buildings located inGoa. In the evening you can very well enjoy the sunset at any beach that you like.

Day 17: Explore Goa and Back to Mumbai. In the morning enjoy water sports like swimming, boat riding, water scooter riding, skiing and other activities on the sea beaches. In afternoon session enjoy shopping at theGoamarket. After that you can fly to Mumbai. Once you are here in this busy city you will get a transfer to the hotel and then a stay over night there.

Day 18: Explore Mumbai. After the breakfast in the morning you will be taken to see the beauty of the various historical sites in Mumbai, in the evening you will get a transfer to the International Airport for departure.
