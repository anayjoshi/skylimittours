Detailed Itinerary

Day-1: Arrive Delhi. On arrival in Delhi, you will be met and by our representative for transfer to your hotel. Overnight: New Delhi

Day-2: Delhi. After breakfast you will be taken a full day sightseeing tour of New Delhi. Visit Qutab Minar, Humayun's tomb, Akshardham - A Hindu Temple, Parliament House, India Gate and Connaught place, the shopping centre of New Delhi. See old Delhi in the evening, Visit the Jama Masjid, one of the largest Mosque of India, Raj Ghat the cremation sites of Mahatama Gandhi. Also drive past Chandni Chowk, -Shopping centre of Old Delhi. We’ll help you learn about the places you visit and the importance they carry, you’ll also be helped with ticketing and entry procedures at all junctures. Overnight: New Delhi

Day-3: Delhi – Jaipur (Train Journey) (265 Kms, 5-6 hrs Drive). Early Morning transfer to railway station to board the train for Jaipur. Arrive Jaipur & transfer to hotel. Hawa Mahal - one of the most famous monuments of Jaipur, Hawa Mahal; the Palace of the wind was built by Maharaja Sawai Pratap Singh in 1799. Designed by Lal Chand Usta, Hawa Mahal is five storey building. Unusual architecture of Hawa Mahal is made of pink and red sand stone elegantly outlined with motif’s and white borders. Amber Fort - Amber Fort reflects the intricate architecture of the artisans of yesteryears that bowl over even the most seasoned traveler. The elephant joyride to the fort makes the visit a fascinating experience. Jantar Mantar - Built by Jai Singh Jantar Mantar was conceptualised with the primary aim of unraveling the mystery of outer space. In the days of yore, Jantar Mantar served the purpose of carrying out astronomical observations and also inspired and motivated many to become interested in the science of astronomy, at a time when the society at large had become entangled in silly superstition and religious bigotry. You can also visit local bazaars for some exclusive traditional jewelry and clothing purchase. Dinner at Chokhi dani (an ethnic village resort) where you will have an idea of the village life of Rajasthan here you will also enjoy various traditional Rajasthani dances, puppet show and various other interesting activities. Overnight: Jaipur

Day-4: Jaipur -Fatehpur Sikri - Agra. Morning drive to Agra via Fatehpur sikri, The Deserted capital of the mughal emperor Akbar, and the shrine of the Sufi Saint Salim Chisti. Overnight: Agra

Day 5: Agra. We start our day with the best, The Taj Mahal, Early morning visit to the Taj Mahal. After Breakfast see the Agra fort, The beautiful buildings inside reflect an interesting synthesis of Hindu and Central Asian architectural styles. The maze of the courtyards, mosques & private chambers of the fort echo the story of the Mughal Empire. The Moti Masjid & other magnificent buildings reflect the skill of the ancient Indian architects. You’ll also find a lot of memorabilia that you can carry back with you. There are local markets that offer wonderful handicrafts and other impressive souvenirs. Overnight: Hotel

Day-6: Agra - Jhansi - Orchha. Morning transfer to railway station to board the Bhopal Shatabadi Express train to Jhansi. Arrive Jhansi & drive to Orchha & transfer to hotel. Found by Rajput chieftain, Radra Pratap in the early 16th century and remained the capital of powerful Rajput Kingdom from 1583 till 1783 Visit the Orchha fort, walk through a series of hindu temples, visit the old bazzar. Overnight: Orchha

Day-7: Orchha - Khajuraho (172 Kms, 3-4 hrs drive). After breakfast drive to Khajuraho. The legendary temples of khajuraho are an uncomparable experience., constructed between 950 and 1050 A.D. during the reign of Chandela Empire these temples are acknowledged as one of the world?s artistic wonders, Originally there were 85 temples, of which only 22 still exist. The murals depict the life and times of the Chandelas, and celebrate the erotic state of being. They not only testify to the mastery of the craftsman, but also to the extraordinary breadth of vision of the Chandela Rajputs under whose reign, these temples were constructed. Arrive Khajuraho & transfer to hotel. Later explore the Western and Eastern temples of khajuraho. These temples are of huge interest as they have some of the most erotic tales sculptured on walls. Overnight: Khajuraho

Day-8: Khajuraho – Varanasi (399 Kms, 6-7 hrs drive). Morning at leisure for independent activities. Transfer to the Airport to board the flight to Varanasi Arrive Varanasi, the holiest place in India,, filled with spiritual and religious aura- , lunch, Start for a excursion to Sarnath, visit museum Enroute (Site Meusum), Visit a Buddhist temple and see a Bodhi Tree. Optional – Evening aarti on the Banks of River Ganges. Overnight: Varanasi

Day-9: Morning visit the ghats , Boat ride, walk through narrow alleys of the ancient city of Varanasi. Visit the most sacred living temple – The Vishwanath Temple, Back to the Hotel for A Yoga Session at your Hotel by a Yoga Guru. After Lunch transfer to the airport to board the flight to Delhi. Our representative will meet you in Delhi and help you to transfer to the International airport for your flight to your onward destination
