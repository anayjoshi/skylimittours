Golden Triangle Classic

Destinations Covered in Tour

(6 Nights / 7 Days)

Delhi (1 nights) > Agra (1 nights) > Jaipur (2 nights) > Delhi (2 nights)

Delhi: The Capital of India

Agra: Famous for TajMahal

Jaipur: The Capital of Rajasthan known as Pink City

Detailed Itinerary

Day-1: Delhi. On arrival in Delhi, you will be met and transferred by our representative to your hotel. Overnight: New Delhi

Day-2: Delhi - Agra (203 Kms, 3-4 hrs Drive). This Morning after breakfast proceed for a half day city New Delhi tour, we will see The India Gat, The Parliament House, The President House, Qutab Minar, and Humayunâ€™s Tomb. The driver and our representative accompanying you will ensure that you can get easy access to these sites and will also help with ticketing arrangements. Afternoon, drive to Agra. On arrival check into hotel in Agra. Overnight: Agra

Day-3: Agra - Jaipur (232 Kms, 4-5 hrs Drive). Morning, half day city tour of Agra We start our day with the best, The Taj Mahal, Early morning visit to the Taj Mahal to the the sunrise. After Breakfast see the Agra fort, The beautiful buildings inside reflect an interesting synthesis of Hindu and Central Asian architectural styles. The maze of the courtyards, mosques & private chambers of the fort echo the story of the Mughal Empire. The Moti Masjid & other magnificent buildings reflect the skill of the ancient Indian architects On the way you can also pick up some marble memorabilia that Agra is famous for. Afternoon drive to Jaipur enroute visit Fatehpur Sikri, the deserted capital of the third Mughal Emperor - Akbar. Arrive Jaipur, check into hotel. Overnight: Jaipur

Day-4: Jaipur. We are in the land of the Maharajas and we start our day with the visit to the Amber fort. Later visit the Jagmandir or the Hall of victory glitterning with mirrors, Maharaja's City Palace and the Observatory and the Hawa Mahal (The wind palace). Overnight: Hotel

Day-5: Jaipur - Delhi (265 Kms, 5-6 hrs Drive). Morning at leisure. You can also choose to spend the time before your next travel for shopping some great Jaipur gemstones or handicrafts or traditional wear. Later in the afternoon drive to Delhi. On arrival at Delhi transfer to your hotel. Overnight: New Delhi

Day-6: Delhi. After breakfast Sight-seeing of Old Delhi, Visit the Red Fort, built by Mughal Emperor Shahjehan, Jama Masjid, one of the biggest Mosque of India, enjoy the rickshaw ride, visit the Raj Ghat, the cremation sites of Mahatama Gandhi. Also drive past Chandni Chowk, we will try to ensure that you get the best brief about the history behind these sites as well. Overnight: New Delhi

Day-7: Delhi. After breakfast transfer to the airport/Railway Station for your onward destination.
