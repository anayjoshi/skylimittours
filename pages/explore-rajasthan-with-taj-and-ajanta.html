Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Arrive Delhi. Arrival to Delhi in the late evening and then you will be transferred to the hotel. Delhi is the capital city of India and it is one of the oldest cities. Spend your night at Delhi hotel.

Day 02: Sightseeing at Delhi. Once you have your morning breakfast you will be taken to visit some famous attractions of Delhi like Qutub Mina, Humayunâ€™s Tomb as well as Red Fort. After being to these places you will have to spend the night in Delhi hotel.

Day 03: Delhi to Agra and Agra sightseeing (210 Kms, 4-5 hrs drive). In the morning your drive will be towards Agra and once you arrive to Agra you will transferred to the hotel. After getting fresh, you will be taken for Agra sightseeing which will comprise of Taj Mahal as well as Agra Fort. After visiting these you will have to spend the night at Agra hotel.

Day 04: Agra to Jaipur (240 Kms, 5-6 hrs drive). Once you have your morning breakfast you will be driven to Jaipur. On the way to Jaipur there are some of the attractions you will be taken to like The Red Sandstone Fatehpur Sikri as well as well known Ghana Bird Sanctuary which is in Bharatpur. When you reach to Jaipur you will be transferred to Jaipur hotel and you will have to spend a night there.

Day 05: Explore Jaipur. When you are done with morning breakfast you will be taken for Jaipur sightseeing which will be comprise of Royal Observatory, Jaipur City Palace, Amber Fort, Palace of Winds (Hawa Mahal) as well as Jaigarh Fort. Some activities while tour: At Amber fort you can take the pleasure of Elephant ride. Shopping: City of Jaipur is well known for bright embroidered fabric, silver jewelry, carpets, blue pottery as well as precious stones. Evening dinner: In the evening take the pleasure of dinner at Chokhi dani which is actually a traditional village resort. This is the place where you can experience actual Rajasthan life. There will be traditional Rajasthani dance, puppets shows and many more such interesting activities.

Day 06: Jaipur to Bikaner (350 kms, 6-7 hrs drive). In the morning your drive will begin towards Bikaner and when you reach this place you will be transferred to the Bikaner Hotel. Spend a night at Bikaner Hotel.

Day 07: Explore Bikaner. There are some of the attractions of Bikaner that are worth visiting which comprises of Lal Garh Palace, Junagarh, Ganga Golden Jubilee Museum as well as Camel Research Farm. Activities during tour: Take the pleasure of Camel Safari. Shopping: Are you fond of shopping? Well then Bikaner is famous for various things which comprises of rangi dupatta, wooden antiques, kundan work, mojari, carpet as well as Lacquer bangles. Another very famous art work of Bikaner is camel hides.

Day 08: Bikaner to Jaisalmer (325 kms, 6-7 hrs drive). Jaisalmer will be your next destination where you will drive to in the morning. On the way to Jaisalmer you will be taken to Karni Mata Temple which is six hundred years old and Gajner Wild Life Sanctuary. In the evening you will reach to Jaisalmer you will be transferred to the hotel. Spend a night at Jaisalmer hotel.

Day 09: Sightseeing at Jaisalmer. Once you have your breakfast you will be taken for Jaisalmer sightseeing. Some of the attractionsyou will be taken to will be Jaisalmer Fort, Gadi Sagar Lake, Jain Temples and Patwon ki Haveli. Spend a night at Jaisalmer Hotel.

Day 10: Explore Jaisalmer. After taking your breakfast your tour will continue to Jaisalmer and you will be taken to Salin Singh Ki Haveli, Sam Sand dunes, Nathmalji Ki Haveli, etc. Spend a night at Jaisalmer hotel. Activities while travelling: At Sam Dunes take the pleasure of camel ride. Shopping: Some fo the famous things that you can shop at Jaisalmer are Rajasthani mirror work, blankets, old stonework, antiques, rugs, embroidered fabric and many more such things. At Kadi Bandar in the north of the city dye as well as other fabric can be availed.

Day 11: Jaisalmer to Jodhpur (270 Kms, 5-6 hrs drive). Jodhpur will be the next destination you will be drove to in the morning itself. Once you reach there some attractions you will be taken to which comprise of Mehrangarh Fort, Jaswant Thada and Ummaid Bhawan Palace. Spend a night at Jodhpur.

Day 12: Jodhpur to Kumbhalgarh (270 kms, 6-7 hrs drive). Once you have your morning breakfast you will be drove to Khumbhalgarh. On the way to this place you will be taken to Ranakpur Temple. Once you reach Kumbhalgarh you will be transferred to the hotel. Spend a night at Kumbhalgarh hotel.

Day 13: Khumbhalgarh to Udaipur (90 kms, 2 hrs drive). Early morning you will be taken to visit attractions like fabulous Kumbhalgarh fort as well as Kumbhalgarh National Park. From there you will be taken to Udaipur and transferred to the hotel where you need to spend one night.

Day 14: Udaipur sightseeing. Once you have your morning breakfast you will be taken to visit various attractions of Udaipur which comprises of City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Ahar, Sajjan Garh, Saheliyon ki Bari, Pichola Lake, Shilpgram, Eklingji Temple as well as Bhartiya Lok Kala Museum. Spend a night at Udaipur Hotel. Activities during travel: Shopping: The people who are fond of shopping can enjoy the innumerable shops as well as their offerings of interesting and wonderful local crafts. Udaipur is famous for paintings mainly of Rajputs and Moghuls styles.

Day 15: Udaipur to Aurangabad. In the morning you will have to fly to Aurangabad through Mumbai. Once you reach Aurangabad you will be transferred to the hotel. Spend a night at Aurangabad Hotel.

Day 16: Explore Aurangabad. Once you have doen with your morning breakfast you will be taken to visit several historical monuments which are at Aurangabad. Take the pleasure of the evening dinner and spend a night at Aurangabad Hotel.

Day 17: Sightseeing at Aurangabad mainly Ajanta cave. Once you have had your morning breakfast you will be taken to Ajanta Cavewhich is near Aurangabad. You will be drove back to Aurangabad in the evening. After that you will have to fly to Mumbai. Get transferred into the hotel and spend a night at Mumbai Hotel.

Day 18: Explore Mumbai. In the morning once you have had your morning breakfast you will be taken to explore various attractions of Mumbai. On top of that you will also be taken to beaches to enjoy beach activities. After that in the evening you will be driven to International airport for departure.
