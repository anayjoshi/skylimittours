Golden Triangle With Ranthambore

Destinations Covered in Tour

(6 Nights / 7 Days)

Delhi (1 night) > Agra (1 night) > Jaipur (2 nights) > Ranthambore (1 night) > Delhi (1 night)

Delhi: The Capital of India

Agra: Famous for TajMahal

Jaipur: The Capital of Rajasthan known as Pink City

Detailed Itinerary

Day-01: Delhi. On arrival in Delhi, you will be met and transferred by our representative to your hotel. The remainder day is for rest and leisure in or around hotel. Overnight: New Delhi

Day-02: Delhi - Agra (203 Kms, 3-4 hrs Drive). This Morning after breakfast proceed for a half day city New Delhi tour, we will see The India Gate, The Parliament House, The President House, Qutab Minar, and Humayun’s Tomb. Throughout your Delhi experience your accomplice will help you through the ticketing and touring of the sites. Afternoon, drive to Agra On arrival check into hotel in Agra.Overnight: Agra

Day-03: Agra - Jaipur (232 Kms, 3-4 hrs Drive). Morning, half day city tour of Agra We start our day with the best, The Taj Mahal, Early morning visit to the Taj Mahal to the the sunrise. After Breakfast see the Agra fort, The beautiful buildings inside reflect an interesting synthesis of Hindu and Central Asian architectural styles. The maze of the courtyards, mosques & private chambers of the fort echo the story of the Mughal Empire. The Moti Masjid & other magnificent buildings reflect the skill of the ancient Indian architects. You’ll also come across various marble handicrafts and other local specialties that you can shop for. Afternoon drive to Jaipur enroute visit Fatehpur Sikri, the deserted capital of the third Mughal Emperor - Akbar. Arrive Jaipur, check into hotel. Overnight: Jaipur

Day-04: Jaipur. We are in the land of the Maharajas and we start our day with the visit to the Amber fort. Later visit the Jagmandir or the Hall of victory glitterning with mirrors, Maharaja's City Palace and the Observatory and the Hawa Mahal (The wind palace). During your stay in jaipur you’ll also get good enough time to shop for gemstones, jewellery and great colorful traditional Indian costumes. Overnight: Jaipur

Day-05: Jaipur – Ranthambore (185 Kms, 3-4 hrs drive). Drive to Ranthambore Day Five will bring you to Sawai Madhopur, where you can check into a forest lodge, on the outskirts of the Ranthambore National Park. Post lunch, enjoy an exhilarating jungle safari inside the wildlife sanctuary and have fun spotting wild animals and exotic birds. Overnight: Ranthambhore

Day-06: Ranthambore. Early morning Safari to see the tigers, other wildlife and the natural beauty of the reserve. After Lunch transfer to the railway station to catch your train to New Delhi Arrive New Delhi – you will be met and transferred by our representative to your hotel.

Day-07 After breakfast Sight-seeing of Old Delhi, Visit the Red Fort, built by Mughal Emperor Shahjehan, Jama Masjid, one of the biggest Mosque of India, enjoy the rickshaw ride, visit the Raj Ghat, the cremation sites of Mahatama Gandhi. Also drive past Chandni Chowk. Overnight: New Delhi transfer to the airport/Railway Station for your onward destination. Golden Triangle with Ranthambore Photo Gallery
