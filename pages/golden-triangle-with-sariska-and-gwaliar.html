Detailed Itinerary

Day-1:: Delhi. On arrival in Delhi, you will be met and transferred by our representative to your hotel. Overnight: New Delhi

Day-2: Old Delhi and New Delhi. After a leisurly breakfast at the hotel. Embark on sightseeing tour of Delhi including Old Delhi and New Delhi. Visit the Red Fort and the Jama Masjid. Visit Chandani Chowk and enjoy rickshaw ride in the narrow street of Old Delhi. Visit the Raj Ghat – the cremation site of Mahatma Gandhi. Visit the Qutub Minar – the longest brick minaret in the world. Visit the India Gate – a war memorial dedicated to unknown soldiers who laid their lives in first world ware for the sake of country. Visit the Humayun’s Tomb – the tomb of the second mughal emperor. Visit the modern temple structures – Birla Temple, Lotus Temple and Akshardham Temples. Visit the government buildings built by the british – the Parliament House and President House. We will ensure that you can get all possible help with a brief guide on all sites that you visit by an accompanying person. Overnight: New Delhi

Day-3: Delhi – Sariska ( 185 Km, 04 hours ). This morning after breakfast drive to Sariska. Upon arrival at Sariska, checkin to your hotel . Sariska is situated in Alwar District of Rajasthan, India. Sariska is world famous for tiger reserve and wildlife tourism. After Lunch, visit the famous Sariska National Park & Tiger Reserve. Enjoy Jeep Safari to watch different varieties of wild animals such as tigers, leopards, panthers, wild boars, antelopes, spotted deer, barking deer, etc. Overnight: Sariska

Day-4: Sariska – Jaipur ( 120 Km, 02.1/2 hours ) This morning after breakfast start your drive to Jaipur (the pink city). Jaipur is a historic city situated in the state of Rajasthan. It is the capital city of Rajasthan. It is. It was founded by the great Rajput ruler – Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh Jaipur is one of the well-planned cities in the country . you will be met and transferred by our representative to your hotel. Evening - a walking tour of the colorful streets and bazaars of Jaipur City, here you can enjoy shopping for fabulous gemstone jewelry as well as great traditional Indian wear as well. Overnight: Jaipur

Day 5: Jaipur We are in the land of the Maharajas and we start our day with the visit to the Amber fort in the outskirt of Jaipur overlooking wonderful view of the city. Enjoy royal elephant ride to ascend the fort complex. Later visit the Jagmandir or the Hall of victory glitterning with mirrors, Maharaja's City Palace and the Observatory (one of the five observatories built by Maharaja Sawai Jai Singh and this is the biggest of the five) and the Hawa Mahal (The wind palace) from where royal ladies used to watch daily processions and life of the city. Overnight: Jaipur

Day-6: Jaipur – FatehpurSikri – Agra ( 235 Km, 4-5 hours ) After breakfast at the hotel leave for Agra visiting en route Fatehpur Sikri en route. Fatehpur Sikri is a walled town built by the third mughal Emperor Akbar in 1569 AD as a ceremonial capital. But unfortunately it was abandoned due to scarcity of water and Akbar went back to Agra. Visit the famous historical sites and the Buland Darwaza at Fatehpur Sikri. On arrival in Agra, a representative assists you to transfer to the hotel. Agra is a historically rich city in India and globally famous for it monuments like Taj Mahal (one of Seven Wonders of World), Agra Red Fort, and Itmad-Ud-Daullah Tomb (the Baby Taj). In the Evening visit the Taj Mahal – a white marble wonder – the most beautiful symbol of love – built by ftifh mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz. See the matchless beauty of Taj Mahal at sunset. Overnight: Agra

Day-7: Agra This morning we see the awesome beauty of Taj Mahal at sunrise from a different prospective we go to the Mehtab Bagh or the Moonlight garden to see the Taj Mahal across the river Yamuna and see the sunrise over the Taj. After Breakfast visit the Agra Fort built by Akbar, the rusty and majestic red-sandstone fort of Agra stands on the banks of the river Yamuna and the construction was started by Emperor Akbar in 1566. After Lunch visit Itmad-Ud-Daullah Tomb which is also famous as the Baby Taj from where Shajahan was ispired to build the Taj mahal in White. You will also get time to take a stroll down the markets and buy some great Taj memorabilia as well as a lot of other things that you’d love to carry as souvenirs. Overnight: Agra

Day-8: Agra - Gwalior – Delhi (Shatabadi Express) – Departure After early morning breakfast, you will be transferred to the Agra Railway Station to board Shatabadi Express Train for Gwalior. Gwalior is an historical town situated in the state of Madhya Pradesh. Upon arrival in Gwalior, you will be met by our representative who will take you for the sightseeing tour of Gwalior. You will visit 15th century Gwalior Fort, the famous temples within fort, and rock-cut Buddhist & Jain statues.

MEMORIAL OF TANSEN AND TOMB OF MOHAMMAD GHAUS - Adjacent to each other are the tomb of Ghaus (a saint of the Islamic faith of the 16th century) and Tansen (a famed musician, and one of the nine gems of Akbar’s court). Visit the Jai Vilas Palace - A European style palace converted into museum, Jai Vilas Palace is situated in Gwalior. Its unique architecture, which is a mix of Italian, Tuscan and Corinthian, is quite amazing. Jai Vilas Palace is constructed by the Maharaja Jiyaji Rao Scindia and the fabulous palace is festooned with furniture from Versailles, Italy and France. In the evening you will be transferred to the Gwalior Railway Station to board same Shatabadi Express Train for New Delhi. Upon arrival in Delhi, our representative transfers to New Delhi International Airport to catch flight for your home/onward destination.
