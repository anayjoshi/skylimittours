Exotic and Classical Rajasthan

Destinations Covered in Tour

(13 nights and 14 days)

Delhi: The Capital of India.(2 nights)

Agra: Famous for one of the seven wonders of world Taj Mahal.(1 night)

Jaipur: The Pink City & Capital of Rajasthan.(2 nights)

Jodhpur: The second Largest city of Rajasthan.(1 night)

Jaisalmer: The Golden Desert City.(3 nights)

Manvaar: The Desert Resort.(1 night) Ranakpur: Famous for Jain Temple.(1 night)

Udaipur: The City of Lakes.(2 nights) Delhi/Mumbai

Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Arrive Delhi. You will reach Delhi in the late evening. After that you will be transferred to the Delhi hotel. Delhi is one of the oldest cities of India and it is also the capital city of India. Spend a night at Delhi Hotel.

Day 02: Sightseeing Delhi. Once you have had your morning breakfast you will be taken to visit some of the attractions of Delhi which comprises of qutub Minar, Red Fort as well as Humayun’s Tomb. Spend a night at Delhi hotel.

Day 03: Delhi to Agra (210 Kms, 4-5 hrs drive) & Explore Agra. In the morning you will be drove to Agra and get transferred to the hotel. Keep your luggage as well as get fresh and then you will be taken to visit magnificient Taj Mahal as well as Agra Fort. Spend a night at Agra.

Day 04: Agra to Jaipur (240 Kms, 5-6 hrs drive). Once you have had your breakfast you will be taken to Jaipur. On the way to Jaipur there are some fo the attratciosn you will be taken that is The Red Sandstone as well as well known Ghana Bird Sanctuary which is in Bharatpur. Once you reach Jaipur you will be taken to the hotel where you need to spend a night.

Day 05: Explore Jaipur. Once you have doen with your breakfast you will be taken for Jaipur sightseeing. This will comprise of various attractions which comprise of Royal Observatory, Amber Fort, Palace of Winds, Jaigarh Fort as well as Jaipur City Palace. Activities during tour: Take the pleasure of elephant ride near Amber fort Shopping: There are some of the items of Jaipur which are famous which comprises of blue pottery, silver jewelry, carpets, embroidered fabric, semi precious stones and many more such things. You can take the pleasure of shopping these items. Dinner in the eveining: Take the pleasure of evening dinner at Choki Dani. This is basically an ethnic village resort wherein you can experience real Rajasthan. You will get to see Rajasthani dance, puppet show and many other interesting activities of Rajasthan.

Day 06: Jaipur to Jodhpur (330 kms - 6 hrs). In the morning get ready to drive to Jodhpur. On the way to Jodhpur there are some of the attractions you will be taken to which comprise of Temple of Lord Brahma which is on the banks of Lake Pushkar. You will see various devotees praying as well as some of them taking dip in the holy water of the lake. Take your lunch at Pushkar and then continue the journey towards Jodhpur. Once you reach there you will be transferred to the hotel. In the evening you will reach Jodhpur then relax and spend a night at the hotel.

Day 07: Explore Jodhpur & move towards Jaisalmer (290 kms - 6 hrs). In the morning you will be taken for Jodhpur sightseeing which comprises fo Jaswant Thada (Mausoleums of the Rajput warrior), Mehrangarh fort as well as Umaid Bhawan Palace Museum. In the afternoon you will be drove to Jaisalmer. Once you reach there you woll be transferred to the hotel wherein you can relax and spend a night in the hotel.

Day 08: Explore Jaisalmer. Once you have done with your morning breakfast you will be taken to sightseeing which comprises of Jaisalmer Fort, Gadi Sagar Lake, Patwon ki Haveli and Jain Temples. Spend a night at jaisalmer Hotel.

Day 09: Sightseeing at Jaisalmer. There are many attractions in Jaisalmer which you can take pleasure of visiting and so you will be taken to some of the places once you have your breakfast. These attratciosn will comprise of Salin Singh Ki Haveli, Nathmalji Ki Haveli, Sam Sand Dunes as wlel as Khuri. Spend a night at Jaisalmer Hotel. Activities during travel: At Sam sand dunes you can take the pleasure of camel ride. Shopping: There are many things that you can shop from Jaisalmer as Jaisalmer is well known for things like stonework, antiques, blankets, Rajasthani mirror work, etc. Kadi Bundar is also a great shop to shop for dye as well as other fabrics which is in the north of the city.

Day 10: Jaisalmer to Manvaar (180 Kms, 3 hrs drive). In the morning get ready to drive to Manvaar. This is the place wherein you will find highest sand suns and it is the place located in the Shergarh Region of Thar Desert. Once you reach at Manvaar Desert in the Jeep you will be drive to camp sites of Manvaar mainly in the dunes. Then you will be driven to Swiss tents. These are the ones that comprises of all the basic amenities which also comprises of attached English toilets. You can get fresh here and then go for unlimited camel safari mainly till the sunset and after that you can go back to the campsite. On this campsite you can take the pleaure of folk dance as well as music and also enjoy the theme dinner. Spend a night at these tents.

Day 11: Manvaar to Ranakpur (220 kms – 4 hours). In the morning you will be transferred to ranakpur. Once you reach there you will be taken to the temples of Ranakpur which were built in twelfth century and it is the one that stands on 1444 pillars which are carved and the best part is that every pillar is carved differently. After this you will be transferred to the hotel. In the evening you can relax and thenspend a night at Ranakpur Hotel.

Day 12: Ranakpur to Khumbhalgarh and then Udaipur (100 kms, 3 hrs drive). Once you have done with the breakfast in the morning you will be drove to Udaipur. On the way to Udaipur you will be taken to visit Khumbhalgarh Fort. After this you will be taken to Udaipur and then transferred to the hotel. Spend a night at Udaipur hotel.

Day 13: Explore Udaipur. Once you have doen with your morning breakfast you will be taken to visit attractions of Udaipur which comprises of Saheliyon ki Bari, Jagdish Temple, Ahar, City Palace, Sajjan Garh, Bharitya Lok Kala Museum, Eklingji Temple, Pichola Lake as well as Shilpgram. Spend night at Udaipur hotel. Activity during travel: Shopping: There are innumeral shops in Udaipur wherein you can enjoy shopping of various local crafts and the famous things here is Rajput as well as Moghuls style paintaings.

Day 14: Back to Delhi/Mumbai. Take a flight to Delhi/Mumbai and get transferred to International airport for departure. Book Exotic and Classical Rajasthan
