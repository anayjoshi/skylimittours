Tour Plan

Day 01: Arrive Delhi and move towards Jaipur Once you reachDelhi, you will be transferred from the international airport to the domestic airport and take a flight to jaipur. After you reach jaipur in the morning, you will check in the hotel. The entire remaining day you can relax or explore thenew city. You can take the pleasure of enjoying delicious dinner at chokhi dhani, a village resort. There you will also be able to witness the traditional life of village and enjoy the Rajasthani folk dance, puppet shoes and mnay other exciting activities.

Day 02: Jaipur to Mandawa (Shekhwati Area) (170 Kms, 4-5 hrs drive) In the daylight hours, you will get the advantage of visiting many places on the way like shekhawati area and nawalgarh, which is well renowed for paintings. after you reach mandawa, you can witness the beauty by sight seeing various havelis such as murmuria haveli, mahan lal saraf haveli and the gouenka haveli. Overnight in mandawa.

Day 03: Mandawa to Bikaner (215 Kms, 4-5 hrs drive) & Explore Bikaner During the sunlight hours, you take a drive to witness the maginificentBikaner. Once you reach there you will be visiting places like junagarh, ganga golden jubilee museum, camel research farm etc. Tour activity Take pleasure from the camel ride. You can enjoy shopping out there too. You can shop for mojari, kundan work, wooden antiques, rangi dupatta, carpet making and many more items

Day 04: Bikaner to Jaisalmer (325 kms, 6-7 hrs drive) In the daybreak, move towards jaisalmer. on the way explore many things like 600 years old karni mata temple and gajner wildlife reserve. you will reach jaisalmer in the evening hours and stay at jaisalmer.

Day 05: Explore Jasialmer After the brunch, you will be taken on a tour to explore jaisalmer and will get the chance to visit jain temples, patwon ki haveli, gadi sagar lake and jaisalmer fort. Overnight in jaisalmer.

Day 06: Discover Jasialmer Past breakfast, continue with the tour on exploring jaisalmer. Visit salin sign ki haveli, nathmalji ki haveli, sam sand dunes and khuri as well. Overnight stay in jaisalmer. Travel doings Camel ride- take pleasure of the camel rides in sam sand dunes. Shopping- jaisalmer is very famous for rugs, rajasthani mirror works, embroidery, blankets, antiques etc.

Day 07: Jaisalmer to Jodhpur (270 Kms, 5-6 hrs drive) & Explore Jodhpur Drive to the second largest city of rajasthan,Jodhpurin the first light. Once you reachJodhpur, you will taken to visit jaswant thada, ummaid bhawan palace and mehrangarh fort. Overnight in Jodhpur.

Day 08: Jodhpur to Udaipur (270 kms, 5-6 hrs drive) In the morning, leave fromjodhpurto explore the city of lakes,Udaipur. While going on the way you will be visiting the magnificent ranakpur jain temple. Transfer to the hotel once you reachUdaipurat night. Night stay in Udaipur.

Day 09: Explore Udaipur Past breakfast, you get the chance to visit the city palace, jagdish temple, ahar,PicholaLake, sajjan garh, saheliyon kibari, shilpgram, bhartiya lok kala museum, eklingji temple. overnioght stay atUdaipur. Tour activity Enjoy shopping asUdaipurhas number of small shops and many fascinating things like local crafts and especially the paintings of the rajputs.

Day 10: Udaipur to Jaipur (430 kms, drive 7-8 hrs.) In the sunlight hours you will drive back to jaipur and on the way will also be taken to chittorgarh for witnessing the renowned chittorgarh fort. Arrive jaipur at night and check in the hotel

Day 11: Explore Jaipur Past breakfast, you will be taken to the places of visit in jaipur like palace of winds, royal observatory, jaipur city palace, jaigarh fort and also amber fort. Travel activity Get pleasure from elephant ride in ambar fort. Shopping Jaipur is well renowed for brightly embroidered fabrics, silver jewellery, carpets, semi-precious stones, unique blue pottery. Enjoy evening dinner at chokhi dhani, a village resort where you will be witnessing and enjoying every moment with traditional rajasthan dances, puppet shows and many other exciting acitivities.
