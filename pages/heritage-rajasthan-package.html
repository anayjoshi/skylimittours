Jodhpur: The Second Largest city of Rajasthan.(1 night)

Kumbhalgarh: Famous for Kumbhalgarh Fort.(1 night)

Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Reach Delhi and move towards Jaipur. Once you reach theDelhiinternational airport, you will be picked up from then and transferred to the domestic airport to catch a flight for jaipur. Once you reach jaipur and check in, in the crack of dawn you can relax the whole day or even move out for shopping. In the nightfall you get the chance to have a feast at Chokhi Dhani cultural township resort. At this place you will actually experience the village life of Rajasthan. With the memorable experience, you will also enjoy traditional dances, puppet shows and many other cultural activities.

Day 02: Jaipur – Mandawa Ride (Shekhwati Area) (170 Kms, 3-4 hrs drive). After enjoying delicious breakfast, move towards Mandawa and while you are on the way you get a chance to visit the semi desert Sekhawati region as well as Nawalgarh. Nawalgarh is famous for excellent paintings. After your arrival in Mandawa, you have tour to Murmuria Haveli, Mahan Lal Saraf Haveli, Binshidhar Nawetia Haveli and Goenka Haveli. Night stay at Mandawa.

Day 03: Mandawa – Bikaner (215 Kms, 4-5 hrs drive) & Explore Bikaner. During the day light hour drive towardsBikaner. once you reachBikanerthere are many place worth visiting like Junagarh,Gangagolden jubilee museum, camel farm. get pleasure from camel safari. you can also go for shopping for mojari, kundan work, rangi dupatta, carpet making and many more things.

Day 04: Bikaner – Jaisalmer Ride (325 kms, 5-6 hrs drive). In the sunlight hours drive towards Jaisalmer, on the way you will be visiting Deshnok as well as Gajner wild life sanctuary. Overnight stay at Jaisalmer.

Day 05: Explore Jaisalmer. Past breakfast you will be visiting Patwon Ki Haveli, Jaisalmer fort, Jain temples andGadiSagarLake. Stay overnight in Jaisalmer.

Day 06: Jasialmer Day II. After brunch you will be taken to witness the beauty of Jaisalmer. You will be visiting Natmalji Ki Haveli, Sam sand dunes, Salim Singh Ki Haveli and Khuri. Travel activity You will be taken to enjoy the camel ride at Sam sand dunes. You can even take pleasure from shopping as Jaisalmer is renowned for embroidery, rugs, blankets, antiques, Rajasthani mirror work etc.

Day 07: Jaisalmer to Jodhpur Ride (270 Kms, 5-6 hrs drive) & Explore Jodhpur. After sunrise travel toJodhpurand once you reach there you will be visiting jaswant thada, mehrangarh fort and ummaid bhavan palace. Stay overnight atJodhpur.

Day 8: Jodhpur to Mt. Abu (270 kms, 5-6 hrs drive). Post breakfast move towardsmountAbu, once you reach there check in to hotel and stay night atmountAbu.

Day 9: Mount Abu - Explore Mount Abu. After brunch, you will be visiting delwara temple, guru sikhar, achalgarh,NakkiLakeas well as sunset point. Overnight stay inmountAbu.

Day 10: Mount Abu to Kumbhalgarh (230 kms, 5-6 hrs drive). After sunrisetravel towards kumbhalgarh, on the way you will be witnessing ranakpur Jain temple. Reach kumbhalgarh at late evening and stay overnight.

Day 11: Explore Kumbhalgarh &move towards Udaipur (90 kms, 2 hrs drive). After breakfast visit the kumbhalgarh fort and then move towardsUdaipur. Once you reachUdaipur, you will be transferred to the hotel.

Day 12: Explore Udaipur. Post breakfast you will be witnessingUdaipurcity palace, jagdish temple, ajar, saheliyon kibari, Pichola lake, bhartiya lok kala museum and eklingji temple. Travel activity Shopping Udaipurhas n number of shops and various exciting local crafts and paintings of the rajputs.

Day 13: Udaipur to Bundi (260 kms, 5-6 hrs drive). Past breakfast you will be traveling to bundi, on the way you will be taken to visit the chittogarh fort. Once you reach bundi, you will visit taragarh fort and stay overnight at bundi.

Day 14: Explore Bundi & travel to Ranthambore (250 kms, 5-6 hrs drive). After breakfast you will be visiting the bundi palace, such mahal, chitrashala and various water tanks. After you finish exploring bundi, drive towards ranthambore.

Day 15: Explore Ranthambore & leave for Jaipur (180 kms, 3-4 hrs drive). After sunrise you will be taken to witness the mesmerizing ranthambore fort and the well renowned ranthambore national park. After your visit, travel towards jaipur and stay night at jaipur.

Day 16: Explore Jaipur. Past breakfast you will be taken to witness the city palace, palace of winds, amber fort, jaigarh fort and royal observatory. Travel activity Enjoy to the fullest the elephant ride at amber fort. Enjoy shopping of brightly coloured fabrics, silver jewelry, semi precious stones and carpets. Enjoy dinner at chokhi dhani, village resort where you will be witnessing traditional dances, puppet show and various other exciting activities.

Day 17: Jaipur to Agra (235 Kms, 5-6 hrs drive). In the daylight drive towardsagraand then on the route visit the well-knownGhanabird sanctuary, bharatpur, fatehpur sikri and red sandstone. After you reachagrayou will be taken to the world famous wonder of the world, taj mahal. Overnight stay atagra.

Day 18: Agra to Delhi (210 Kms, 4-5 hrs drive). Past brunch you will be visiting the famousagrafort and then drive towardsdelhi. Onve you reachdelhi, you will be taken to visit the qutub minar, the red fort as well as humayuns tomb. Here your tour ends and then you will transfer to the international airport.
